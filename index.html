2. Data Collected by the App

WeathAff does not require account creation and does not collect personally identifiable information such as your name, phone number, or email address during normal use.

To operate, the app may process the following information:

  • Name of the city searched by the user
  • Cities saved to favorites
  • Last searched city (to restore on reopen)
  • Device coordinates or approximate location, only if the user explicitly grants permission
  • Technical data required to query external weather services (e.g. city name, coordinates)
📍

3. Geolocation

WeathAff may request access to your device's location solely to display weather forecasts for your current position.

This permission is entirely optional. If you decline, the app continues to work normally and you can search for any city manually.

Your coordinates are sent directly to Open-Meteo to retrieve weather data and are never stored on the developer's servers.

💾

4. Local Storage on Your Device

Some preferences are saved locally on your device using the webview's localStorage, including:

  • Cities saved to favorites
  • Last city viewed
  • Interface settings and preferences
This data stays on your device and is never transmitted to any server operated by the developer. You can delete it at any time by clearing the app's data or uninstalling WeathAff.
🌐

5. Third-Party Services

To function properly, WeathAff makes requests to external services. These services may receive technical data such as your IP address, user agent, and network information, in accordance with their own privacy policies.

  • Open-Meteo (open-meteo.com) — geocoding, reverse geocoding, and weather forecasts. Open-source service with no advertising tracking.
  • Google Fonts — loading the typeface used in the interface.
  • D3.js and TopoJSON — libraries loaded from CDN for rendering the interactive globe view.
⚖️

6. Purpose and Legal Basis

Data is processed exclusively for the following purposes:

  • Displaying weather forecasts and information for the selected location
  • Enabling city search and favorites management
  • Showing weather for your current location, if permission has been granted
  • Ensuring the correct technical operation of the app

The legal basis for processing is performance of a contract (Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR) — i.e., delivering the service you requested — and the developer's legitimate interest in keeping the app working correctly. For geolocation, the legal basis is your explicit consent (Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR).

🔁

7. Data Sharing

The developer does not sell, rent, or share personal data with third parties for commercial or advertising purposes.

The only technical data transmitted is what is strictly necessary to query the weather and geocoding services listed in section 5, in accordance with their respective privacy policies.

🗂️

8. Data Retention

Locally stored data remains on your device until you manually delete it, clear the app's data, or uninstall WeathAff.

As there is no proprietary backend, the developer does not hold copies of users' personal data on any servers.

🛡️

9. Your Rights (GDPR)

As a user in the European Economic Area, you have the right to:

  • Access — obtain confirmation of whether your data is being processed and a copy of it
  • Rectification — correct inaccurate data about you
  • Erasure ("right to be forgotten") — request deletion of your data
  • Restriction — limit processing of your data in certain circumstances
  • Objection — object to processing based on legitimate interest
  • Withdrawal of consent — revoke location permission at any time via device settings
  • Complaint — lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority (e.g. in Italy: Garante per la Protezione dei Dati Personali)

To exercise any of these rights, contact the developer at weathaff@tommyzaffa.com.

👦

10. Children's Privacy

WeathAff is not designed to knowingly collect personal data from children under the age of 16. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us so we can arrange its removal.

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time to reflect changes to the app or applicable regulations. Material changes will be communicated by updating the date at the top of this page.
